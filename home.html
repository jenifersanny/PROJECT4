Our Cultural Heritage

Traditional Kolos

The Kolo is a traditional male garment worn during cultural ceremonies and special occasions. Each Kolo is handwoven using traditional techniques passed down through generations, featuring intricate patterns that tell stories of our ancestors.

Meri Blouses

Our Meri blouses represent the elegance and grace of PNG women. These beautiful garments are crafted with attention to detail, incorporating traditional designs that have been cherished for centuries.
